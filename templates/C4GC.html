What is C4GC?

-C4GC is a mobile application that allow users to report something that is undesirable things, conduct or Gordon College's property within the school premises.

Why C4GC?

-it stands for Care For Gordon College. Everyone who go to Gordon College to work, to study etc. should care for the institution. How? by reporting flaws of the School and making it a better place.

What does this app contribute?

-C4GC allows users,student,guest,or employee to report to the admin for quicker notice, quicker repairement and quicker action.

What can you report?

- Broken things
- Students behavior
- Suggestions
